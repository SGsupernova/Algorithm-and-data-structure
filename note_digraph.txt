Type ctrl+v and

subscript : u2081 (subscript 1 / ₁) , u2082 (subscript 2)
superscript : u00b2 (superscript 2 / ²)


or

Type ctrl+k
plus minus ± : +-
Omega Ω : W*
subscript zero : 0s
superscript zero : 0S
